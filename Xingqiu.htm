Personal Review and Comment on Xingqiu

Xingqiu is one the best hydro and support character in Genshin Impact in my opinion. He is a good support during fights especially that his elemental burst provides consistent Hydro application. The reason why I rate him as "SS" is because even though he is just a four-star character, he is overpowered and his elemental skill provides multiple elemental particle.
